Privacy Policy (प्राइवेसी पॉलिसी)

Last Updated: June 2026

LuminaEdu par aapka swagat hai. Hum apne users ki privacy ka poora sammaan karte hain. Yeh Privacy Policy batati hai ki jab aap hamari website use karte hain, toh hum aapki jankari ko kaise surakshit rakhte hain.

1. Jankari Jo Hum Ikatha Karte Hain

Hum apne users se kisi bhi tarah ka **Sensitive Personal Data** bina unki saspasht anumati (explicit permission) ke ikatha nahi karte hain. Agar aap hamare contributor program me join karte hain, toh hum sirf aapka naam aur email address hi save karte hain.

2. Cookies aur Web Beacons

Hamari website website ke performance ko behtar banane aur traffic analysis ke liye cookies ka upayog kar sakti hai. Iske alawa, bhavishya me Google AdSense ke dwara ads dikhane ke liye third-party cookies ka upayog kiya ja sakta hai jo aapke interest ke anusar ads dikhane me madad karti hain.

3. Data Suraksha (Data Security)

Aapka data hamare paas poori tarah surakshit hai. Hum aapki kisi bhi niji jankari ko kisi teesre paksh (third-party) ke sath na toh bechte hain aur na hi share karte hain.

4. Privacy Policy me Badlav

Hum samay-samay par apni policy me badlav kar sakte hain. Kisi bhi badlav ki jankari aapko isi page par mil jayegi.
